Summary:
@dr_dragon found a bug in our partners application that resulted in an open redirect. When sending a request to test an app on a development store, the install_app[store_id] parameter lacked validation, allowing its value to be changed to any store.
#steps to reproduce:
1-Open your shopify partner account.
2-Create an app and click on test your app.
3-Select a development store you own.
4-Intercept the request using burpsuite and change the "install_app[Select a store]" parameter to any store  with no validation.
